Our Story

How a class project became a perfume label

Noor Atelier started as a university studio assignment where we had to design a physical product and a simple web experience around it. We were three friends who loved fragrance but could not find local blends that felt modern and personal.

Instead of copying luxury trends, we focused on memories we knew: rainy evenings, old books, chai breaks, and polished wood desks. That is still our approach today - small batches, clear compositions, and scents that feel close to real life.

Over time, that class project evolved into a growing community-led brand. We test every formula in real everyday settings - classrooms, offices, events, and family gatherings - to make sure the scent performs naturally and comfortably in our climate.

Our Mission

Design accessible luxury perfumes with clear character and long-lasting comfort.

Our Process

Small-batch blending, wear testing, then careful final bottling before launch.

Our Promise

Consistent quality, transparent scent profiles, and respectful customer support.
